This FAQ provides clear, non‑negotiable guidance for implementing attendance and engagement interventions using Panorama.

1. The Big Rule: How do I start an intervention?

First decision: Is the student enrolled in OFSDP?

YES

Track MINUTES

NO

Track DAYS

⚠️ If this decision is wrong, the intervention will be wrong. Do not proceed until this is clear.
2. What SHOULD counselors do in Panorama?
  • ✓ Use Panorama to document attendance interventions for identified students.
  • ✓ Apply one monitoring method only (days or minutes, never both).
  • ✓ Use district‑established thresholds consistently.
  • ✓ Progress monitor students at regular intervals.
3. What are the common MISTAKES to avoid?
  • ✕ Do not track minutes for non‑OFSDP students.
  • ✕ Do not track days for OFSDP students.
  • ✕ Do not mix ADA periods and class‑by‑class tracking.
  • ✕ Do not create duplicate or overlapping interventions.
  • ✕ Do not wait for perfection before entering data.
4. How does Progress Monitoring work in plain language?

Non-OFSDP Students

Goal: Number of Days in Grading Period

Action: Track DAYS present.

OFSDP Students

Goal: Minutes earned tied to the OFSDP structure

Action: Track MINUTES earned.

Note: Panorama is used for monitoring trends, not recreating Skyward.

5. Do I track ADA only or all periods?

Track days, not individual periods, unless the student is OFSDP.

6. Can minutes be pulled automatically from Skyward?

No.

Minutes must be entered manually. They cannot be pulled automatically from Skyward or Tableau at this time.

7. What if I’m unsure I’m doing it right?

Pause and ask. Confirmation is encouraged rather than guessing, as incorrect data entry affects intervention data integrity.
